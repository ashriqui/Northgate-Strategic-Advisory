What We Do

A government sales
function.
Without the overhead.

A dedicated government sales director costs $130K+ annually. We provide the same capability — relationships, pipeline management, bid execution — at a fraction of the cost, with no employment risk and no learning curve.

01

Government Sales Leadership

We run your government sales end to end. Vendor registration, capability positioning, procurement relationships, standing offer applications, bid responses. You get coverage and capability — not a report telling you what to do.

02

Market Entry

For manufacturers with zero government presence, we build the foundation from scratch. The right opportunities, the right registrations, the right introductions — in the right order.

03

Bid & Tender Execution

When the right opportunity appears, we own the response. Positioning, writing, submission. We've been through this process and we know what wins.

04

Prime Contractor Access

Some of the best government revenue doesn't come from bidding directly — it comes from becoming a sub-supplier to a prime with existing standing offers. We know who they are and how to get in front of them.

Our Approach

Execution,
not advice.

Most government BD engagements produce strategy decks and contact lists. We produce pipeline activity, standing offer applications, and contract wins. The distinction matters — government sales requires consistent presence and real relationships, not a playbook handed over at the end of an engagement.

We work as an embedded function, not an external advisor. In the office when it matters. In Ottawa when it matters. At trade shows when it matters.

01

Intelligence & Positioning

Map the specific contracts and standing offers your business is eligible for. Identify the procurement officers and prime contractors who matter. Build the vendor registration and capability foundation.

02

Active Outreach

Direct introductions. Standing offer applications. Bid responses. RFP tracking. We run the activity that government sales actually requires — not the activity that looks good in a status update.

03

Pipeline & Close

Government procurement moves slowly. We keep the pipeline moving, maintain relationships between opportunities, and close when the moment is right. First contract is the foundation for everything that follows.
